About

ResearchAI turns important questions into structured professional reports.

ResearchAI is a research workspace for founders, operators, analysts, students, creators, and independent professionals who need clearer decisions without starting from a blank page.

What ResearchAI Does

ResearchAI helps transform a question, plan, market, product idea, comparison, or learning goal into a structured report with executive summary, findings, analysis, risks, recommendations, source guidance, and limitations.

Who It Is For

ResearchAI is built for people who make decisions: early-stage founders validating ideas, consultants preparing client-facing work, operators exploring markets, students structuring learning plans, and professionals comparing options.

Current Limitations

ResearchAI is in public beta. Reports should be treated as structured first drafts, not verified final research. Source verification, citations, team workspaces, richer exports, and account-based storage are still being expanded.

Mission

Our mission is to make high-quality research work more accessible, more structured, and more useful. We believe AI products should help people produce work worth saving, reviewing, and improving.
