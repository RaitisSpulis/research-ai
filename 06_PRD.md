# Product Definition

ResearchAI is a professional research platform that transforms one user question into structured, actionable work.

ResearchAI is NOT a chatbot.

ResearchAI is NOT a search engine.

ResearchAI is NOT an AI wrapper.

ResearchAI is a productivity platform.

Its purpose is to eliminate hours of manual research.

---

# Product Goal

Reduce professional research from hours to minutes.

Every feature exists to help users complete meaningful work faster.
