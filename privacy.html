Privacy Policy

Your reports should feel useful, not mysterious.

Last updated: July 8, 2026. This policy explains what ResearchAI collects, where reports are stored, and how AI generation works.

Local Browser Storage

ResearchAI stores saved reports, pinned reports, and usage counters in your browser using localStorage. This lets the workspace remember your recent reports on the same device and browser. Clearing browser data may remove saved reports.

Gemini Generation

When live AI generation is active, your prompt and structured request details may be sent to the ResearchAI backend and then to Gemini for report generation. Do not submit confidential, regulated, or highly sensitive information unless you are comfortable using AI generation for that content.

Report Storage

Reports are currently saved locally in your browser. ResearchAI does not provide account-based cloud storage in this public beta. Exports you create are stored wherever you choose to save them.

Analytics

ResearchAI may use privacy-conscious analytics to understand product usage, performance, errors, and conversion. Analytics should not be used to read the content of your locally saved reports.

Cookies

ResearchAI does not require cookies for the core local workspace. If analytics, security, or hosting tools use cookies in the future, they will be used to operate and improve the service.

User Data

We use user data to provide the product, improve reliability, respond to support requests, and protect the service. We do not sell personal information. For privacy questions, contact support@researchai.app.
